About the Artist

Anjelina Villalobos is an American painter whose work explores the complex interplay between nature's organic forms and the surreal landscapes of the human psyche. Her meticulous attention to detail and command of color create immersive worlds that are both familiar and fantastical.

She has been featured in publications like Hi-Fructose and Juxtapoz, with solo shows in New York, Los Angeles, and London.
