Design Evaluation

Option A: Thumbnail Grid

Visual recognition - see artworks at a glance
Flexible view options (grid/list toggle)
Compact - fits more artworks visible at once
Clean, modern gallery aesthetic
Artwork counts provide context
Grid may feel cramped on narrow screens
Requires good quality thumbnails
Design Score: 8/10 - Practical & Elegant

Option B: Vertical Timeline

Tells a chronological story
Sticky year headers keep context visible
Larger thumbnails = better detail
Smooth scrolling experience
Unique, memorable design
Takes more vertical space per artwork
More scrolling required
Design Score: 9/10 - Elegant & Story-Driven
