Recommended Solution: Design 10 (No Contact Section)

The cleanest fix is to remove or relocate the contact section from the mobile artwork view. The social links can be accessed via the About page. This solves the overlap completely while maintaining the immersive artwork viewing experience.

Alternative: Design 7 (Collapsible) if you want to keep the contact section but give users control over when they see it.
